How It Works

MASTER FORMULA

Risk Score =

(Amount Risk × 0.25) +
(Location Risk × 0.25) +
(Time Risk × 0.15) +
(Merchant Risk × 0.20) +
(Velocity Risk × 0.15)
Amount Risk (25%)

Compares transaction to your average spending. Higher amounts = higher risk.

min(100, (amount / avgSpend) × 20)
Location Risk (25%)

Measures distance from your home location. Farther = higher risk.

min(100, distance / 20)
Time Risk (15%)

Transactions between 12 AM - 5 AM are flagged as unusual.

(hour >= 0 && hour <= 5) ? 80 : 10
Merchant Risk (20%)

Certain categories (gambling, crypto, etc.) have higher base risk.

categoryRiskScores[merchant.category]
Velocity Risk (15%)

Multiple transactions in short time period increases risk.

min(100, recentTxnCount × 15)

Threshold Settings

🟢 0-39: Low Risk | 🟡 40-69: Medium | 🔴 70-100: High
